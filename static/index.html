Our Notable Alumni!

Akhilesh Yadav

Akhilesh Yadav is the former chief minister of Uttar Pradesh (UP). He is the son of politician Mulayam Singh Yadav and Malti Devi. His first significant success in politics was his election as a member of the Lok Sabha for the constituency of Kannauj in 2000. He obtained his Bachelor's and master's degrees in Civil Environmental Engineering from JSS Science and Technology University(SJCE). He also has a master's degree in environmental engineering from the University of Sydney, Australia.

Javagal Srinath

Javagal Srinath, born on 31 August 1969, is a former Indian cricketer and currently an ICC match referee. He is considered among India's finest fast bowlers, and is the first Indian fast bowler to take more than 300 wickets in One Day Internationals. He obtained his Bachelor's degree in Instrumentation Engineering from JSS Science and Technology University(SJCE).

Vijay Prakash

Vijay Prakash is an Indian singer and music composer. He has given his voice for Hindi films such as Blue, Yuvvraaj, Swades, Kaal, Lakshya, Matrubhoomi, Tere Naam, Cheeni Kum, Raavan and Force. He has also performed in Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam and Marathi movies. He is one of the judges for the Kannada reality show Sa Re Ga Ma Pa on Zee Kannada. He has also performed shows with Zakir Hussain. He won the Karnataka Government's 'Best Playback Singer' award for the year 2016 for the song "Nammooralli Chaligaladalli" from the film Beautiful Manasugalu.
